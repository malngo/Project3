Human Development in ASEAN

The Association of Southeast Asian Nations (ASEAN) consists of Brunei Darussalam, Myanmar/Burma, Cambodia, Indonesia,Laos, Malaysia, Philippines, Singapore, Thailand, and Vietnam. Southeast Asian countries have a wide range of HDI values. Throughout the years, these countries have been steadily rising in HDI, while others are still underdeveloped.
